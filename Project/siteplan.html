Bassey Freelancing Site Plan

Site Name

Bassey Freelancing

This name was chosen to clearly reflect the site's purpose of connecting freelancers with clients seeking small-scale, quick-turnaround projects.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to provide a platform where freelancers can access short-term microjobs posted by clients, enabling quick employment opportunities and efficient service delivery.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: What is the best way to find freelancers to complete my data entry task?

Scenario 2: What are the payment terms and conditions for completing a microjob?

Color Schema

The color scheme for the site will include:

  • Green (#4CAF50) for headings and accents
  • White (#f5f5f5) for background and buttons

Typography

The typography for the site will include:

  • Roboto for headings
  • Open Sans for body text
